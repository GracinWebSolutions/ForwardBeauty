 About Me

 Hi, I’m Briana Gracin, and I’m so excited to have you in my chair!

 I’m a graduate of Franklin Hair Academy, certified in Trust and Mane extensions and Brazilian blowouts. I specialize in extensions, lived-in color, blondes, and big, transformative looks that help people feel like the best version of themselves.

 My mission is simple: I want every person who sits in my chair to leave feeling beautiful, confident, and radiant. Whether you’re dreaming of longer hair, a fresh blonde, or a total color transformation, I’m here to create a personalized, relaxing experience tailored just for you.

 You might notice the phrase Forward Beauty around here—that name means everything to me. It’s inspired by a quote that’s been a source of reassurance throughout my life: “Fail forward.” To me, failing forward means embracing every misstep as a chance to grow, to learn, and to keep moving. It’s about becoming better—not in spite of failure, but because of it.

 That’s the spirit I bring into my work. I believe beauty is a journey, not a destination, and it’s okay if we’ve stumbled along the way. What matters is how we rise, how we heal, and how we move forward—together.

 I can’t wait to meet you and help bring your hair goals to life.
Book with me today—and let’s create something beautiful.
